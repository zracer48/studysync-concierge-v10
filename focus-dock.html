Product Features

Built around real study habits

The Focus Dock is designed for students who need a simple place to keep core study materials visible, accessible, and connected to their weekly plan.

📱 Adjustable Phone StandPositions the phone for planner access without letting it take over the workspace.
📒 Notebook / Tablet SlotHolds notebooks, tablets, folders, or planners in a central location.
✏️ Pen OrganizerKeeps pens, highlighters, and markers upright and easy to reach.
📝 Whiteboard StripCreates a quick space for reminders, deadlines, and study priorities.
🗂️ Flashcard HolderSupports review cards and short study sessions without spreading materials across the desk.
🔌 Cable ManagementRoutes charging cables neatly to reduce clutter and improve the study setup.
