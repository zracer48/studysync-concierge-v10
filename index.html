How StudySync Concierge Works

Students move from the landing page into the validated software MVP, generate a prioritized plan, optionally join the waitlist, and then submit feedback. This final feasibility version reflects 20 completed tester responses.

👥

1. Discover

Student finds StudySync through social media, QR code, group chat, or referral.

💻

2. Visit Landing Page

Student reviews the value proposition and clicks the main CTA.

🚀

3. Launch MVP

Student opens the software MVP and enters basic academic information.

🗓️

4. Generate Plan

The tool creates a prioritized study schedule, workload summary, and accountability plan.

💬

5. Join / Give Feedback

Student can join the early-access list and click the feedback button inside the MVP.

📈

6. Improve

Feedback is used to improve pricing, features, and future testing.

Physical Product MVP

StudySync Focus Dock brings the planner into the workspace

The Focus Dock is a physical study station designed to reduce desk clutter and keep the most important study tools in one place. It extends the StudySync concept beyond software by combining organization, reminders, phone placement, cable management, and direct QR access to the planner.
